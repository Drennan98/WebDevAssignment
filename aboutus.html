Our Legacy

Celebrating 70 years of championship golf and community.

Since 1952

More Than Just a Game

What started as a humble 9-hole layout on the edge of the county has grown into the region's premier golf destination. Designed by the legendary architect Arthur Hills, our course was built on the philosophy that golf should be a walk through nature.

Over the decades, we have hosted regional championships, charity galas, and countless Sunday morning rounds between friends. We are dedicated to stewardship of the land and the traditions of the game.
